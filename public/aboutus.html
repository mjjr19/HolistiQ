Revolutionary AI-Powered Health Coach

HolistiQ is more than just another fitness app. We're a revolutionary AI-powered health coach that truly understands your unique background, preferences, and goals. Our platform bridges the gap between technology and personal wellness by incorporating cultural awareness into every aspect of your health journey.

Unlike traditional health apps that offer generic, one-size-fits-all solutions, HolistiQ challenges outdated fitness myths and restrictive dieting approaches. We believe in sustainable lifestyle changes backed by science, not temporary fixes or unrealistic regimens.

Our Story

How four MSIS students with diverse cultural backgrounds created HolistiQ

January 2025

The Beginning

Four MSIS students at the Foster School of Business—Mubasheer, Simran, Emily, and Vanroth—connect through their shared passion for technology and wellness.

January 2025

Identifying the Problem

Through personal experiences and market research, the team identifies significant pain points in the health and wellness industry: lack of cultural awareness, generic recommendations, and the prevalence of ineffective "bro-science" advice.

January 2025

The Spark

In Professor Leo's class, they're assigned a project to leverage machine learning and AI to solve a real-world problem. The team decides to tackle the wellness industry's shortcomings head-on.

February 2025

Concept in Class Presentation

The first HolistiQ prototype is developed, combining AI-driven recommendations with culturally-aware nutrition guidance and evidence-based fitness advice. The team presents their idea to the class with high-level professional judges who gave them critical feedback.

February 2025

Back to the drawing board

After receiving positive and critical feedback, the team goes back to the drawing board and leveraging new knowledge acquired in class, comes up with the features that will define HolistiQ.

March 2025

HolistiQ Launches

HolistiQ, its full concept is presented to a classroom full of Professional Judges, leaders of AI, from various high-level companies. HolistiQ is set to launch, bringing AI-powered, culturally-aware health coaching to users worldwide with a mission to redefine wellness through technology and inclusivity.

Our Mission

At HolistiQ, our mission is to redefine wellness through AI-powered, culturally inclusive health coaching. We believe that true well-being is holistic, encompassing nutrition, fitness, and mental health while respecting the unique lifestyles, cultures, and traditions of every individual. We leverage cutting-edge AI to create personalized health solutions that empower you to thrive—without restrictive diets, generic fitness plans, or one-size-fits-all approaches. We’re here to help you feel good about yourself, eat the foods you love, and build sustainable habits that enhance your life. Say goodbye to bro-science and hello to science-backed, culturally intelligent wellness.

Inclusivity

We celebrate cultural diversity and design our platform to be accessible and relevant to people from all backgrounds and traditions.

AI Personalization

We leverage cutting-edge artificial intelligence to create truly personalized experiences that evolve with your unique journey and needs.

Science Over Trends

We base our recommendations on evidence-based research and proven methodologies, not passing fads or unsubstantiated claims.

Empowerment

Sustainability
